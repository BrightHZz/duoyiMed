## Supplementary Figure S2 (panel B). Decision curve analysis.

Decision curve showing the net benefit of the PSA-free XGBoost model across threshold probabilities from 1% to 50%. The model provides net clinical benefit over the "treat all" and "treat none" strategies for threshold probabilities between approximately 5% and 40%. The greatest net benefit was observed in the elective admission subgroup. The y-axis represents the net benefit (proportion of true positives minus weighted false positives).

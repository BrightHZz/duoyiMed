## Supplementary Figure S2 (panel A). Calibration plot.

Calibration plot comparing predicted probabilities against observed outcome frequencies for the PSA-free XGBoost model (n = 2,437). The uncalibrated model (red) shows a calibration slope of 0.82, indicating slight overfitting. Points represent deciles of predicted risk. The diagonal dotted line represents perfect calibration (slope = 1.0). Brier score = 0.1339 from 5-fold cross-validation.

# Bimagrumab for Obesity and Type 2 Diabetes — Phase 2 RCT

Heymsfield SB, Coleman LA, Miller R, et al. *JAMA Netw Open*. 2021;4(3):e213426.

## 关键发现

- **设计**: 双盲 RCT, 48周, 75人 (37 bimagrumab, 38 安慰剂)
- **脂肪量**: −20.5% (−7.5 kg) vs −0.5%, P<0.001
- **瘦体重**: +3.6% (1.70 kg) vs −0.8%, P<0.001
- **HbA1c**: −0.76 vs −0.04 pp, P=0.005
- **剂量**: IV 10 mg/kg up to 1200 mg q4w

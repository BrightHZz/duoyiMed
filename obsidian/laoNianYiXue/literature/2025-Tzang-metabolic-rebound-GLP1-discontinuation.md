# Metabolic Rebound After GLP-1 RA Discontinuation

Tzang CC, Wu PH, Luo CA, et al. *EClinicalMedicine*. 2025;90:103680.

## 关键发现

- **设计**: 系统综述+Meta分析, 18 RCTs, N=3,771
- **肥胖人群体重反弹**: +5.63 kg (95% CI 3.52-7.73)
- **T2DM人群体重反弹**: +2.03 kg (95% CI 1.63-2.42)
- **司美格鲁肽 vs 利拉鲁肽**: 8.21 kg vs 4.29 kg
- **停药>26周**: 反弹7.31 kg vs ≤26周 2.51 kg
- **HbA1c**: 肥胖 +0.25%, T2DM +0.65%
